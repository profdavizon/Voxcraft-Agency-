Privacy Policy

VoxCraft Agency is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal details: Full name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number provided when placing an order or making an enquiry.
  • Order information: Details of the services you request, deadlines, formatting preferences, and any uploaded documents or files.
  • Payment details: M-Pesa transaction codes, payment amounts, and payment proof screenshots. We do not store card numbers.
  • Communication records: WhatsApp messages and emails exchanged for order management and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders on time.
  • To send you order confirmations, progress updates, and completed work.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send promotional offers only if you have opted in (you may opt out any time).
  • To improve our services based on your feedback.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may only be shared:

  • With the writer assigned to your project (name and order details only — no contact info).
  • When required by Kenyan law or a court order.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including password-protected admin access, secure communication channels, and limited staff access to client data. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil your order and comply with legal obligations, typically up to 2 years. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data held by us.
  • Right to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to request deletion of your data.
  • Right to opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at: [email protected] or WhatsApp +254 724 850 318.

Refund Policy

Client satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a fair and transparent refund policy to ensure you feel secure when ordering with VoxCraft Agency.

1. Full Refund Eligibility

You are entitled to a full refund if:

  • You cancel your order within 1 hour of placement and before work has begun.
  • We are unable to deliver your order due to circumstances on our end.
  • The delivered work is completely unrelated to your stated requirements (verified case).

2. Partial Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a partial refund if:

  • Work has already begun but you cancel before completion — refund covers the unworked portion.
  • Delivery was significantly late beyond the agreed deadline due to our fault.
  • The work partially meets requirements despite multiple revision attempts.

3. No Refund Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The work has been delivered and meets the stated requirements, but the client has a change of mind.
  • The client provided incorrect or incomplete instructions and the work was done accordingly.
  • A refund request is made more than 7 days after delivery.
  • The work was used or submitted before raising a complaint.

4. Free Revisions (Before Refund)

Before any refund is considered, we offer unlimited free revisions to ensure the work meets your requirements. We strongly encourage clients to request revisions first.

5. Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days via the original payment method (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or bank transfer). To initiate a refund, contact us at [email protected].

6. Digital Services (Websites & Apps)

For tech services (websites, apps, systems): a 50% deposit is non-refundable once development has commenced. The remaining 50% is refundable if delivery completely fails.

Delivery Policy

We are committed to delivering all work on time, every time. Our delivery timelines are clearly communicated at the point of order.

1. Standard Delivery Timelines

  • Essays & Assignments: 24–48 hours depending on length.
  • Research Papers: 48–72 hours.
  • Dissertations / Theses: Per chapter — 3–7 days per chapter.
  • Business Proposals: 2–5 days.
  • CV & Cover Letters: 12–24 hours.
  • Blog Articles: 12–24 hours per article.
  • Websites (Basic): 3–5 working days.
  • Business Systems: 14–28 working days.
  • Mobile Apps: 30–60 working days.

2. Rush Orders

Rush delivery (12 hours or less) is available for writing services subject to writer availability. Rush orders attract a surcharge of up to 50%. Please contact us on WhatsApp to confirm availability before placing a rush order.

3. Delivery Method

  • Completed work is delivered via WhatsApp, Email, or Google Drive as agreed.
  • File formats include .docx, .pdf, .xlsx, or as specified by the client.
  • Tech deliverables (websites, apps) are delivered via live links, GitHub, or direct installation.

4. Delays

If a delay occurs due to our fault, we will notify you immediately and offer a revised timeline. Significant delays entitle you to a partial refund per our refund policy. Delays caused by the client (late payment, missing materials) will shift the delivery deadline accordingly.

5. Revisions After Delivery

All clients are entitled to unlimited free revisions within 7 days of delivery. Revision requests beyond 7 days may be treated as new orders.
